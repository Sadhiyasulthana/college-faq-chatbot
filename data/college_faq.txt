COLLEGE RULES & FAQs

Attendance:
Students must maintain at least 75% attendance to be eligible for exams.

Examinations:
Internal exams are conducted twice per semester.
Final semester exams are conducted by the university.

Library Rules:
Students can borrow up to 3 books for 14 days.
Late return fine is ₹5 per day per book.

Dress Code:
Students must wear formal attire from Monday to Thursday.
Friday is casual day.

Fee Payment:
Fees must be paid before the 10th of every semester.
Late payment results in a ₹500 fine.

Holidays:
The college remains closed on Sundays and government holidays.
